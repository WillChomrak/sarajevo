top 3 Sights

Latin Bridge

This 16th Century Ottoman bridge is the site of the event that sparked the first World War. The assasination of the Archduke Franz Ferdinand.

Trebevic mountain

The location of many of the 1984 Olympic events such as bobsledding, this was a major battleground in the 1992 - 1995 Bosnian War. Beware undetonated landmines.

Bascarsija

Bascarsija is Sarajevo's old bazaar. Built in the 15th century when the town was founded, it is the historic and cultural center of the town.

Top 3 foods

Cevapi

This grilled minced meat dish is served with a pita, chopped onions, and cream cheese. Cevapi is a national dish of Bosnia.

Dolma

Dolma is meat or rice stuffed into eggplant, zucchini, or bell peppers. Meat dolma comes with a lemon-based sauce, while rice dolma is served with a garlic-based sauce.

Begova Corba

Begova Corba is a traditional soup made of chicken and vegetables. It is typically served as an appetizer.
